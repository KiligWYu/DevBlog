    一个绕过新闻网站付费墙的工具，原理该网站也说了，实际上很简单，就是这些新闻网站对谷歌爬虫放行，这样就可以被搜索到，该工具就是获取谷歌对其网页的缓存。我试了一下端传媒，果然可以。

    News sites want Google to index their content so it shows up in search results. So they don’t show a paywall to the Google crawler.
    Google crawler will cache a copy of the site every time it crawls it.
    All we do is show you that cached, unpaywalled version of the page.
